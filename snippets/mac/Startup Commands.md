Startup commands for [[macOS]] maintenance

## Reset NVRAM, PRAM

### NVRAM
Hardware control
* Shut down Mac
* Press and release power button
* Hold `option` + `command` + `P` + `R`
* Hold for ~20s, or until mac boots twice
